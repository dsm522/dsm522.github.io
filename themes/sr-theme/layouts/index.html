Background

About Me

I've spent my career at the intersection of technology and getting things done — spotting paradigm shifts early, getting hands-on fast, and figuring out what they mean for real organisations.

It started in the mid-90s: building C++ GUI apps for SITA on ISDN terminals. I could see the internet was about to change everything, so I jumped to a startup on nothing more than "I'm a fast learner" — and spent the next 7 years building payment systems, music streaming, and video platforms before most people had broadband.

I moved into delivery and programme leadership, spending 15 years at the BBC managing portfolios across iPlayer, Sounds, News and Sport — scaling agile across 100+ teams. Along the way, I worked with early AI, delivering NLP and text mining solutions at Nstein Technologies back in 2008.

Now I'm going through that same paradigm shift again — this time with agentic AI. Not the theoretical kind. I deploy AI agents that build applications, manage workflows, and operate autonomously. Currently completing AI & Business Transformation at Imperial Business School.

What I bring:

  • Hands-on credibility — I build with AI daily
  • Organisational fluency — I make new tech work inside real businesses
  • Delivery rigour — 20+ years of making complex things ship
